Key Data Flow Relationships

MAS (Marketing)
CRM (Sales)
ERP (Invoicing)

Lead-to-Deal-to-Invoice Flow

  • Website forms capture leads and create contacts in HubSpot
  • Lead scoring and nurturing qualify MQLs to SQLs
  • Sales team works deals through pipeline stages
  • Closed Won deals sync to Zoho for invoice generation
  • Invoice data syncs back to HubSpot for visibility
Closed Won Deal
Auto-Create Renewal
90-Day Alerts

Automated Renewal Management

  • Workflow creates renewal deal immediately on Closed Won
  • Time-based automation moves deals through renewal pipeline
  • Alerts trigger at 90, 60, and 30 days before renewal
  • Successful renewals chain-create next year's renewal
New Customer
Onboarding
Health Monitoring

Customer Success Lifecycle

  • Account phase tracks Onboarding → Adoption → Growth
  • Sentiment monitoring (Green/Yellow/Red) with alerts
  • NPS surveys via in-app or email integration
  • At-risk playbooks trigger for declining health scores
  • Upsell opportunities identified through usage data
Support Ticket
PSA Project
Billing Output

Support to Services Flow

  • Support cases in Jira transitioning to HubSpot Service Hub
  • PSA projects track implementation and professional services
  • Time tracking feeds into billing calculations
  • Billing output connects to CXT Billing Server
All Systems
Power BI
Dashboards & Reports

Analytics and Reporting

  • Data aggregation from all systems into Power BI
  • 4DX Dashboard for wildly important goals
  • QBR KPIs for quarterly business reviews
  • Monthly reporting packs for stakeholders
  • Future migration consideration to MS 365
